Question

Why do I need “ACCFIN SERVICES” if we have a full fledged Accounts Department?

Answer 

“ACCFIN SERVICES” is professional in accounts writing and financial management. Your Accounts Department are professionals in maintaining your accounts, financial statement preparation and maintaining other accounting work. But some times asking them to assist your company in strategic planning and management is a little like asking your architect to build your own building. 

Question 

Why do we need a “ACCFIN SERVICES” if our company has a Financial Controller?

Answer

You probably do not need “ACCFIN SERVICES “ if your Financial Controller  provides you with timely, accurate, and insightful financial and operational information about your business; then he is the person you trust to help you create and update your strategic plans; also can help you assess your organizational efficiency and is helping you to lead your company to the next level. He has assembled a talented and professional accounting staff and can put together a financial package, effectively present it to your banker, and get the financing you need. But if your Financial Controller cannot do these things, “ACCFIN SERVICES” can do it for you.

Question
