Financial Management Services

ACCFIN also provides the below financial management services to our Client Companies

  1. Supervising and Managing the Accounts Department staff.
  2. Get missing accounting entries entered in the books and updated fast.
  3. Ensuring all revenue earned and expenses incurred during the period has been recorded properly.
  4. Maintaining chart of accounts.
  5. Issuing sales invoices quickly and promptly.
  6. Analyze Accounts Receivable Reports and Follow up & Collection on time.
  7. Pay Accounts Payable on time and take appropriate discounts on it.
  8. Calculating job costs.
  9. Checking monthly bank reconciliation statements.
  10. Getting the payroll processed.
  11. Getting passed all the required accrual entries, closing entries at the month and year end.
  12. Reconciliation of all balance sheet accounts.
  13. Fast closing of the year end accounts.
  14. Issuing useful management reports every month and at the year end.
  15. Issuing yearend financial statements.
  16. Assisting with the annual audits getting done.
  17. Managing the budgeting process by collecting the information from all the departments.

Many businesses may not hire a Financial Controller / Manager because the salary cost is prohibitive. Because of this, many Small and Medium sized business may not have the Finance expertise and guidance for their business.

Our scalable business model allows you to leverage Financial Controller /Manager services when needed during key periods and then scale back this service during non-peak periods, at your discretion. And it can be customized to fit your needs
