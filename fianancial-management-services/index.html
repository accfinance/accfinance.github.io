FINANCIAL MANAGEMENT SERVICES

(Finance Manager & Controller Services)

We also provide the below financial management services to our Client Companies.

Our Finance Manager and Controller Services provide you with access to knowledgeable CA level expertise when your business demands it. Let our professional and experienced team of financial experts perform your company’s Finance Manager & Controller level functions when they are required. Many businesses may not hire a Financial Manager & Controller because the salary is cost prohibitive. With this function missing, many businesses lack the expertise to prepare, forecast and analyze operating budgets. Our Finance Manager & Controller level services can be customized to fit your needs and can include the following:

  1. Maintaining chart of accounts.
  2. Supervising and Managing the Accounts Department’s staff.
  3. Get missing accounting entries entered in the books and updated fast.
  4. Ensuring all revenue earned and expenses incurred during the period has been recorded properly.
  5. Issuing sales invoices quickly and promptly.
  6. Analyze Accounts Receivable Reports and Follow up & Collection on time.
  7. Pay Accounts Payable on time and take appropriate discounts on it.
  8. Calculating job costs.
  9. Completing monthly bank reconciliation statements.
  10. Getting the payroll processed.
  11. Getting passed all the required accrual entries, closing entries at the month and year end.
  12. Reconciliation of all balance sheet accounts.
  13. Fast closing of the year end accounts.
  14. Issuing management reports every month and at the year end.
  15. Issuing yearend financial statements.
  16. Assisting with the annual audits.
  17. Managing the budgeting process by collecting the information from all the departments.
  18. Maintain the policies and procedures

Our scalable business model allows you to leverage Financial Manager & Controller services when needed during key periods and then scale back this service during non-peak periods, at your discretion. And it can be customized to fit your needs.
