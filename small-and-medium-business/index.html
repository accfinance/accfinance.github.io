ACCOUNTS WRITING SERVICES

(for Small and Medium sized companies, start ups & Cooperative Housing Societies)

If you are running an established small or medium sized business/ industry, or are launching a start-up business or you are running the management of a cooperative housing society you have more important things to do than to keep your organization’s books. We take care of your accounting books for you, so you can get back to the job of running your business and generating profits and managing your organization.

And also if you feel it is costly to maintain an Accounts and Finance Department or if you are not satisfied with your Accounting or Finance staff and you feel they are unable to keep pace with the work flow.

We can provide the most efficient accounting solutions at cost-effective rates. Our Accounts &Finance Services will help you reduce costs and manage your books of accounts efficiently.

We maintain the Accounts of our client companies / housing societies and can manage your whole Accounts Department on well known popular accounting software programs like QuickBooks Online accounting package and Tally accounting package and other popular accounting applications.

Each month or quarter we'll do the following things for you...

  • Recording Vendor Bills and Payments
  • Customer Invoicing and Collections
  • Passing Journal Book Entries
  • Passing Cash - Bank Book Entries
  • Recording Inventory Transactions
  • Reconciliing of Bank Accounts
  • Passing year end accrual and adjustment entries
  • At year end closing and balancing your Books of Accounts
  • Generate and prepare an Income statement
  • Generate and prepare a Balance Sheet
  • Prepare Cash Flow statement
  • Clean up your yearly General Ledger
  • Provide unlimited consultations

We deliver timely and accurate data and reports so to help move your business forward.
